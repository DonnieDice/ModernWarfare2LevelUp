# ⬛ RGX | Modern Warfare 2 Level-Up! ## ![](https://github.com/donniedice/SimpleQuestPlates/blob/main/images/kiwi.gif?raw=true) RGX Mods - RealmGX Community Project *** ## 🎯 Overview **RGX | Modern Warfare 2 Level-Up!** brings a Modern Warfare 2-inspired level-up sound to World of Warcraft, replacing the default sound with a more tactical, arcade-style level-up hit. ![RealmGX Kiwi](https://github.com/donniedice/SimpleQuestPlates/blob/main/images/kiwi.gif?raw=true) **The Kiwi Says:** "Tactical ding confirmed. Bwwiiiee." *** ## ✨ Features 🎵 MW2 Sound - A Modern Warfare 2-inspired level-up sound plays every time you gain a level. 🎚️ Quality Options - Switch between High, Medium, and Low quality variants. 🔇 Smart Muting - Auto-mutes WoW's default level-up sound so you only hear MW2LU. 💬 Login Message - A branded MW2LU login message appears on login and can be toggled on or off. 💾 Persistent Settings - Settings are saved between sessions. ⚡ Lightweight - Minimal footprint — no embedded libraries required. 🎮 Multi-Version - Includes support for Retail, Wrath, Burning Crusade, and Classic Era. **Note:** All quality variants are included — no additional downloads required. *** ## 📋 Slash Commands The addon can be managed using the following slash commands:
/mw2lu help # Show all available commands
/mw2lu test # Play test sound
/mw2lu enable # Enable the addon
/mw2lu disable # Disable the addon
/mw2lu welcome # Toggle login message
/mw2lu high # Use high quality sound
/mw2lu med # Use medium quality sound (default)
/mw2lu low # Use low quality sound
*** ## 🎮 Compatibility Retail | ✅ Supported Wrath Classic | ✅ Supported Burning Crusade Classic | ✅ Supported Classic Era | ✅ Supported *** ## 📥 Installation 1. **Download** RGX | MW2LU from your preferred platform: * [GitHub](https://github.com/DonnieDice/ModernWarfare2LevelUp) 2. **Extract** to your WoW `Interface/AddOns` directory for your version: * **Retail WoW:** World of Warcraft/retail/Interface/AddOns * **Classic/Wrath:** World of Warcraft/classic/Interface/AddOns * **Classic Era:** World of Warcraft/classic_era/Interface/AddOns 3. **Restart** WoW and enable the addon in the AddOns menu. ![RealmGX Kiwi](https://github.com/donniedice/SimpleQuestPlates/blob/main/images/kiwi.gif?raw=true) **The Kiwi Says:** "Installation is easy!
